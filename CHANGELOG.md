# Changelog

This project uses conceptual versions to distinguish major stages in the development of PBMM.

## v0.4 — Integration and formal structure

- Added a canonical specification.
- Distinguished six-dimensional configuration, three-domain direction, and domain intensity.
- Clarified governance, state, habitual configuration, and attractor.
- Added Bayesian analysis principles.
- Reframed archetypes as regions and masks rather than fixed identities.
- Added integration, capture, psychological fragmentation, and social pathology.
- Added stronger objections and a staged research program.
- Marked several archetype names as provisional.

## v0.3 — Vector and Bayesian interpretation

- Introduced conceptual vector-space language.
- Distinguished priority magnitude and direction.
- Framed person analysis as probabilistic updating rather than categorical typing.
- Emphasized tradeoffs as evidence of priority order.

## v0.2 — Eighteen archetypal directions

- Paired three establishing priorities with three realizing priorities.
- Distinguished governing from supporting priority.
- Generated nine pairings and eighteen directions of governance.
- Developed archetypes as masks and attentional programs.

## v0.1 — Three domains and six priorities

- Defined Value, Stability, and Resources.
- Associated them symbolically with Spirit, Nervous System, and Body.
- Defined Dignity, Recognition, Security, Harmony, Acquisition, and Comfort.
- Introduced masculine and feminine as provisional symbolic directions.
