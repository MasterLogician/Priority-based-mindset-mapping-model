# Priority-Based Mindset Mapping

**Priority-Based Mindset Mapping (PBMM)** is a framework for understanding how people, groups, and institutions organize perception and action around competing priorities.

Its central premise is:

> **A mindset is a geometry of priorities with direction and magnitude.**

People may care about many of the same things yet behave very differently because those things are weighted, ordered, and combined differently.

PBMM compresses this motivational field into three domains:

| Domain | Human layer | Establishing direction | Realizing direction |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Value** | Spirit | **Dignity** | **Recognition** |
| **Stability** | Nervous system | **Security** | **Harmony** |
| **Resources** | Body | **Acquisition** | **Comfort** |

The establishing directions have been described as masculine: they establish, separate, defend, extend, or obtain. The realizing directions have been described as feminine: they receive, relate, integrate, cultivate, reflect, or convert potential into lived experience. These are symbolic directions present in every person, not rigid classifications of men and women.

## The basic idea

Every person contains all six priorities.

A person may have strong dignity, moderate recognition, high acquisition, low comfort, moderate security, and high harmony. The relationship among those weights forms a priority configuration.

PBMM is interested especially in what a person notices first, what they instinctively protect, what they pursue without prompting, what loss most destabilizes them, and what they sacrifice when priorities conflict.

Tradeoffs often reveal priority order more clearly than stated beliefs.

## Archetypes as compressed configurations

Recurring priority configurations can be represented by archetypal masks.

A mask is not a permanent personality type. It is a culturally compressed attentional and behavioral program: a recognizable way of organizing the world around a governing priority and a supporting priority.

For example:

- **Dignity governing Recognition** produces the **King** region.
- **Recognition governing Dignity** produces the **Queen** region.
- **Acquisition governing Recognition** produces the **Fool** region.
- **Recognition governing Acquisition** produces the **Trickster** region.

The full system contains nine cross-polar pairings and eighteen directions of governance. The structural pairing matters more than the current label.

## Integration

PBMM is not mainly a typing system. Its deeper concern is integration.

A healthy individual can access multiple priority functions and move between masks as reality requires. Pathology begins when one priority or archetypal function attempts to become the whole personality, rejects correction, and subordinates every other function.

> **The unintegrated psyche becomes psychologically captured. The unintegrated society externalizes its partial archetypes as ideological factions struggling for total rule.**

A function becomes pathological when it ceases serving the whole and attempts to become the whole.
